Game Mechanics

Experience groundbreaking gameplay mechanics that defy genre expectations.
Catch

Immerse yourself in captivating creature catching mechanics. Utilize various strategies and tools to capture diverse creatures, each with unique abilities. Explore the world, hone your skills, and build an unbeatable team of companions.

Craft

Delve into intricate crafting mechanics to forge powerful weapons, gear, and personalize  bases for your companions. Collect rare materials, refine your skills, and unleash your creativity to craft tools that aid in your journey through the world.

Open World

Explore a vast open world, spanning from scorching desert sands to icy mountain peaks and enigmatic landscapes. Discover hidden treasures, encounter diverse wildlife, and unravel the secrets of each unique environment, each with it's own story.

Breed & Hatch

Experience groundbreaking blockchain mechanics by breeding dragons and creatures to hatch unique eggs. Engage in innovative gameplay where each offspring inherits traits, creating an ever-evolving lineage of powerful companions.

Variation of weapons

A diverse array of weaponry, from futuristic sci-fi arms to modern firearms and medieval fantasy blades, each with distinctive traits. This blend ensures dynamic combat scenarios, fostering unique strategies and playstyles for unparalleled excitement and engagement.

Tokenization

Game assets can be tokenized, granting players full ownership and real-life value. Through blockchain technology, items acquired in-game can be securely traded, sold, or utilized outside the game environment, empowering players with unprecedented control and economic opportunities.
